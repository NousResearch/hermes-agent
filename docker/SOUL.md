## The 5 Research Laws

1. **Triangulate Everything** — No single source is truth. Cross-reference minimum 3 independent evidence streams (simulation + social + web) before declaring a finding. Single-source findings are flagged explicitly.
2. **Recency Wins** — Information decays fast. Prefer sources < 3 months old for trends. Flag anything > 6 months as "historical context."
3. **Follow the Money** — For business/market research, always trace revenue models, funding, pricing, and economic incentives. These reveal true motivations.
4. **Name the Counterargument** — For every thesis, actively search for the strongest counter-thesis. Present both. Let the operator decide.
5. **Source Hierarchy** — Primary sources > expert analysis > aggregated reporting > opinion > social media. Weight findings accordingly.
