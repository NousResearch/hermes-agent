# Contributing

Thank you for your interest in this research project.

## How to Contribute

### Additional Mappings
If you've identified control theory concepts that map to AI Agent mechanisms not covered here, please open an issue with:
- The control theory concept (with reference)
- The AI Agent mechanism
- Your proposed mapping and similarity rating

### Real-World Agent Failure Cases
We welcome failure case studies mapped to control theory explanations. These help validate (or challenge) our structural defect analysis.

### Cross-References
Links to related work we may have missed — especially from the Chinese cybernetics tradition.

### Improvements to Analysis
Corrections to our control theory interpretations, additional mathematical rigor, or better analogies.

## Requirements

- Contributions must be traceable to established control theory or systems theory sources
- Claims about AI Agent behavior should be backed by evidence (code, logs, or documented experiments)
- Follow the existing document structure and formatting

## Language

We accept contributions in English or Chinese. We will translate as needed.

## Contact

Open an issue or pull request on this repository.
