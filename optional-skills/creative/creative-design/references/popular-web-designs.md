## Design System Deep Dives

### Stripe
Signature purple gradients, weight-300 (ultra-light) typography, pill buttons with soft radius, generous whitespace, signature `<fieldset>` patterns for form groups. The gold standard for developer-facing SaaS.

### Linear
Ultra-minimal dark-mode with near-black backgrounds (`#13131a`), ice-purple accent (`#8b5cf6`), extreme precision in spacing (4px baseline), minimal border radius (6px), glassy overlays. Best-in-class dark mode reference.

### Vercel
Pure black (`#000`) and pure white (`#fff`) with zero gray ambiguity. Geist font family. 8px grid. Minimal borders — depth via spacing not shadows. Precision engineering aesthetic.

### Anthropic Claude
Warm parchment canvas (`#f5f4ed`), terracotta accent (`#c96442`), Anthropic Serif headlines with tight 1.10–1.20 line-heights. The most emotionally distinctive design in the catalog — deliberately warm and un-tech.

### Figma
Vibrant multi-color using their signature palette (`#f24e1e` orange, `#ff7262` coral, `#a259ff` purple, `#1abcfe` cyan, `#0ac83b` green). Playful rounded corners (8-12px), generous spacing, friendly but professional.

### Superhuman
Deep near-black (`#1a1a1a`), purple glow effects, premium keyboard-first UI, tight density, custom scrollbars, focus rings that feel like spotlighting. Extreme dark luxury.

### Apple
SF Pro typography, generous white space, cinematic hero imagery, minimal color palette, rounded-rect buttons, premium micro-interactions. Reference for luxury consumer products.

### ElevenLabs
Void-black backgrounds (`#0a0a0f`), purple accent (`#8b5cf6`), audio-waveform visual motifs, cinematic gradients, minimal chrome. Dark mode AI audio product at its most polished.

### Notion
Warm white surfaces (`#ffffff`), dark charcoal text (`#37352f`), subtle borders, generous padding, serif headings (Georgia fallback), no gradients, no shadows by default. The anti-design design system — warmth through restraint.

### SpaceX
Stark black (`#000`) and white (`#fff`) with full-bleed photography, minimal text, futuristic typography. Zero ornamentation — power through reduction.
