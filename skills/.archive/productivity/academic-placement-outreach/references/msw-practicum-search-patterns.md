# MSW Practicum Search Patterns

- Approved school/ELC/Tevera sites should be searched before broad public web leads.
- Addiction, mental health, trauma, IOP/PHP, outpatient, and young-adult programs are strong fits for clinical MSW placement when supervision is available.
- Direct emails to clinical directors/training coordinators outperform generic contact forms, but call generic admissions numbers to find the right coordinator when needed.
- Large hospitals, county agencies, and school districts may be excellent placements but can be slower unless already approved.
- Track every lead with status, next action, follow-up date, contact info, fit, supervision notes, and approval status.
