# Practicum Outreach Email Scripts

Subject: MSW practicum placement inquiry for Fall [Year]

Hi [Name/Team],

My name is [Name], and I’m an online MSW student at [School] based in [City]. I’m looking for a practicum placement starting this fall and wanted to ask whether [Agency] has any opportunities for MSW practicum students.

I’m especially interested in [practice areas]. My background includes [brief relevant experience].

If you are not the right contact, would you be willing to point me toward whoever coordinates practicum students, clinical interns, or training placements?

Thank you,
[Name]
[School/Program]
[Email]
[Phone]
