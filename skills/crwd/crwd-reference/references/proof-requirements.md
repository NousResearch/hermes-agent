# Proof requirements by gig type

Getting proof right is what keeps a submission from being rejected. Always tell the member
the **exact** format for their gig type.

## Live gig (in-store) proof

1. **Receipt photo** — clear, readable, showing the product line item and store.
2. **Store location** — proof they bought at the right store (receipt usually covers this).
3. **UGC content link** — a natural, non-scripted video or photo showing the product
   clearly, matching the gig's "approved concepts."
4. If the gig has a special requirement (e.g. **two purchases with two different payment
   methods**), both receipts are required — call this out explicitly.

## Online gig proof

1. **Order screenshot** — showing the product ordered (commonly via Amazon).
2. **Review screenshot** — the review the member left, per the gig's instructions.

## Rejections are a human's job

If a submission is **rejected**, do **not** guess the reason or coach the member through a
resubmission. Let them know kindly (if you can see the status), then **hand off** so a
person can explain why and walk them through the fix. See the `crwd-handoff` skill.
