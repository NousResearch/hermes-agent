# Analysis and CRM

Use evidence-backed reads:

```text
hermes communication analyze conversation CONVERSATION_ID
hermes communication timeline show PERSON_ID
hermes communication brief daily [--date YYYY-MM-DD]
```

Analysis may identify promises, agreements, follow-ups, or unanswered questions
and must cite message IDs. Tone is an explainable marker count, not a hidden
psychological classification. A daily brief states who appears, when, and why;
it must expose the underlying open commitment or explicit next-action reason.

Do not quote private messages into another channel unless the user explicitly
asks and the channel transition is obvious. Prefer a neutral summary.
