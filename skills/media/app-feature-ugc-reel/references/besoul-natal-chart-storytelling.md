# BeSoul natal-chart storytelling pattern (45s, English)

Use this when the feature is **AI Birth Chart / Natal Chart** and goal is emotional identification + curiosity.

## Core messaging guardrails
- Lead with a **strong pattern-interrupt hook** in the first sentence.
- Contrast: **zodiac sign = broad label** vs **natal chart = deeper personal map**.
- Stay empathetic, non-dogmatic, non-clinical.
- Avoid generic motivational filler.
- Create curiosity with an open loop ("why this keeps repeating", "what I was missing").

## Recommended 3-beat script shape (45s / 3x15)
1) **HOOK (15s)**
   - Example angle: "Your zodiac sign might describe your vibe, but it doesn't explain your patterns."
   - Personal friction moment + emotional relevance.

2) **REVEAL (15s)**
   - Mention BeSoul AI natal chart as the turning point.
   - Explain *why* it's deeper: planetary placements, houses, timing context.
   - One concrete realization the user can identify with.

3) **CLOSER (15s)**
   - Honest limitation line: not magic / not therapy / not absolute truth.
   - Value line: it's a mirror for self-understanding.
   - Soft CTA: "Try your natal chart in BeSoul and see what resonates."

## Copy constraints
- Language: English (if requested).
- No hype clichés: "game changer", "obsessed", "you NEED this".
- No greeting openers ("hey guys", "today I want to...").
- Keep tone calm-intimate, confessional.

## Hook bank (approved style)
- "Your zodiac sign is a headline. Your natal chart is the full story."
- "I thought my sign explained me—until my natal chart explained my patterns."
- "If your sign feels too generic, this is probably why."
